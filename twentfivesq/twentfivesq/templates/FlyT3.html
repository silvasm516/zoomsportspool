    INSTRUCTIONS

    1). Start by taking note of the 'Grids' outstanding box at the upper left. If you have grids remaining, a value greater than zero should appear. Otherwise, you will need to select the 'Buy Games' option. Once you have grids available, select your teams from the dropdown menus. One 'Home' and one 'Visitor' per grid from the 'Pro', 'College', or choose to create your own teams from the 'Custom' categories. Press the green 'Submit Teams' button.

    2). Next, provide an authorization code password that your clients will use to log in with. Then fill out the rest of the form. Press the blue 'Submit Grid' button. Each grid will be noted in the Active Grid Display Panel below. You will need to contact your clients and direct them to the Site. The Row/Column numbers are selected at random, at grid creation, but are not revealed until grid closing at midnight before game day. The grid will remain available for viewing, however, for game day and approx. one extra day.
